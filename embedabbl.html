About

Embedabbl is an easy to use interactive media embeding wrapper made to quickly add Flash Games & Emulators to your website without the need for heavy documentation. Just change a few URL parameters and slap it in an iFrame.

"This is Stupid."
I guess it is. Most of these tools have their own way to embed things. But I made a tool that makes things easier for myself, and I shared it.
