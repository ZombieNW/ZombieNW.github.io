Welcome, to Project Bismuth

Project Bismuth, shortly, is quick and easy fun with friends. The pick up and play lobby system allows players to quickly join lobbies with their friends or other players and start.

The primary development focus was originally to deliver a simple base experience, but now we're looking into adding more content and refining the experience to make this your friend group's latest addition to the game shelf
