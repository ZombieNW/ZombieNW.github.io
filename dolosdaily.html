Dolos Daily

About

Dolos Daily is an AI-Powered fake news website made for educational/demonstrational purposes. The website was developed in SvelteKit and Node.JS. It uses LLAMA-Node to generate news articles and the Unsplash API to find thumbnails. The article subjects are pulled from a static list of topics.
