Smplr Chess

About

This game has existed for almost thousands of years and it's still so darn complicated to play it on your phone? If you want an app that's just chess, here it is! No accounts, no ads, no fuss. Open the app and start playing instantly. Adjust CPU difficulty, get hints (and control their difficulty), and customize themes (coming soon) with an easy-to-use menu. It's straightforward and always just chess.
